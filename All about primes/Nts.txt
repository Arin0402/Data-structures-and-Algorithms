2 Facts about prime numbers

    - two is the only even prime
    - every prime number can be written as 6n - 1 or 6n + 1
    - GoldBach conjecture: Every even integer greater than 2 can be expressed as a sum of two primes
    - 2 and 3 are the only consecutive numbers that are prime
    - Wilson's theorem : (n-1)! % n == (n-1) % n  { where n is a natural number}

3 Definition of a prime number
    - a number which is divisible by 1 and itself and the the numer of factors are 2.

4. The maximum number of prime factors a number can have is log2(n) (i.e log base 2 n)
